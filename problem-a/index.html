Seita

This is Seita from "Grave of the Fireflies". He is one of the two main characters living in Japan during WWII as they try to survive after their home village was firebombed. He is young, but must ensure the survival of himself and his younger sister until their dad returns from his post with the Japanese military.

  • He is resilient in the face of adversity
  • He does everything he can to make his younger sister happy
  • He works hard to ensure they have what they need
